TED,TEDx and TEDx JSSATE

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 30 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. The two annual TED Conferences invite the world's leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less. Many of these talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Sal Khan and Daniel Kahneman.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

Jagadguru Sri Shivarathreeshwara Academy of Technical Education (JSSATE) is an educational institute situated in Noida, Uttar Pradesh affiliated to Abdul Kalam Technical University, Gautam Buddha Nagar. The institute was inaugurated by former president of India Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam in 1998. Having completed 19 years of existence, it continues to be a house of culturally diverse students. TEDxJSSATE 2018 brought to the stage some of the most accomplished pioneers in the arts, technology, medicine and social sciences for an incomparable day of short talks and world-class performances.

RJ Heena

RJ at RED FM 93.5

RJ Heena is a radio jockey at RED FM 93.5 Delhi. She did her B. Comm from Jammu University and further did her Diploma in Radio from IIMC. She then interned at BIG FM Gwalior and got employed full time at RED FM Delhi. Currently, she hosts the show Midnight Masala from 9 PM to 12 midnight. She is very much admired for her show segment where she conveys messages of people while keeping them anonymous. Her content and style have made her one of the favorites in the youth listeners.

The Google Boy

Born in a small village in the district of Karnal, Kautilya Pandit has been cited as the Indian 'Google Boy' because of his exceptional knowledge of subjects that are far beyond a normal child of his age group. Many named psychologist have been observing him to understand his massive memory capacity. Kautilyas talent took him to famous places and people. He appeared in KBC alongside Amitabh Bachchan and also met with the former President Late Dr APJ Abdul Kalam.

Lt Cdr Pravin Tulpule

Happy, the Caring Clown

If it's raining, look for rainbows. If it's dark, look for the stars'. Words befitting Mr. Pravin Tulpule and how he lives and inspires others to live. He is that lissome finger, lingering around to guide everyone around him to look around for rainbows and stars. After serving the Indian Navy for 17 years and retiring as Lt. Commander Communication Specialist, he is reliving life as 'Pintoo-the clown' and has made it his goal to go to orphanages and pediatric wards to spread smiles on the children's faces. Whether it's a magic trick to leave you in a bind of wonder or a trick to make your stomach hurt from laughing, Pintoo has it all.

Tania Singh

CEO, Make Love Not Scar

Tania Singh has been working for more than 2.5 years to help acid attack survivors get back on their feet. Aashqeen

Youtube Content Creator

Making people laugh sure is hard, but for him it almost seems second nature now. Amassing over 100 million views on his YouTube channel, Aashqeen has been hitting his viewers' funny bone since 2015. Chaar Hazaari

Folk Rock Band

What happens when four college students happen to dream of enlarging the scintilla of music within themselves? CHAR HAZAARI. A band created by four people, while pursuing their education; with nothing but their dreams and passion; Char Hazaari was formed in 2012. With music as their panacea, Char Hazaari started their jams and practice sessions, later on adding another member. Infusing classical music with contemporary, Char Hazaari has created nine compositions so far, with many more to come. In their words, they share a dream to play, breathe music as long and as much as they can. From their first gig at Jaypee College, they have surely come a long way with their music being featured in Tehelka Music Project and multiple media platforms. Five young men who were once fighting dreamers, who have now become huge sensations.

Akshara Damle

Psychologist

It could take years to understand oneself, to come to terms with our shortcomings. Understanding someone else, could be another panoply of emotions and experiences. Akshara Damle, however, seems to have gotten the hang of it now. Founder and psychologist at Mano Samvaada, Damle is a life coach, NLP practitioner and a public policy analyst. He also has a keen interest in Indian classical art forms which translates into his works as well.

Parikshit Joshi

Writer and Director

People prefer to be an ace of one rather than a jack of all, but things change just a bit when you want to be the ace of all. Interests in many fields can lead to great failures, but also a greater success, proven such by writer, actor and director, Parikshit Joshi. You can do anything you put your mind to, be it one or many and that is what Joshi has been doing amazingly. Belonging to Almora, Joshi graduated in mass communication at Maharaja Agrasen College. He is the former writer and editor of Mimaansa and has written and directed videos for many YouTube channels, like The Screen Patti, The Timeliners, Girliyapa, The Viral Fever and more. Apart from that, he has also acted in many web series belonging to the above channels, one of it being TSP's Rabish ki Report. Investing his time in creating halcyon content online and offline, Joshi has been efficient in all his fields of work.

Jay Alani

Paranormal Investigator

Some base it on belief, others base it on science. Some refuse to believe out of fear, some out of the argument that it lacks logic. Whatever the case might be, this topic has always been one to gather people's attention. However, it attracted Jay Alani in a way slightly different from others. Instead of playing make-believe with the tales of ghosts, he inclined towards learning about them, and their existence. This gave rise to the beginning of his now 8 year long career in studying paranormal activities. Representing 6 paranormal troops of India internationally, having investigated more than 40 of such cases and having studied over 110 of them, Alani is now all set to publish his own book under Penguin India, which is all set to come out this December.

IPS DR. AJAY PAL SHARMA

SSP, GAUTAM BUDH NAGAR

Born in Punjab, Dr Ajay Pal Sharma is the current SSP of Gautam Budhh Nagar. Originally a doctor, Sharma became an IPS Officer in 2011 from UP Cadre. Having an amazing track record of shutting down organized crimes, eliminating mafia gangs and having expertise in handling kidnapping cases, he has become one of the youth icons of the country. He has also stressed on cyber crimes in the area, and is working on prevention of cyber frauds and fake call centers. His sincerity towards his work has also found its way to praises of the mainstream media.
